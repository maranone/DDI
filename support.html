DDI Support

Last updated: May 2, 2026

This page provides support information for DDI, a dice progression game with a companion app.

Contact

For support questions, contact: ddi.app.contact@gmail.com.

Common Topics

  • Make sure both devices are updated and nearby if companion sync is delayed.
  • In-app purchases are handled by the platform purchase system. If a purchase does not appear, try Restore Purchases in the app.
  • Game progress is stored locally on your devices.

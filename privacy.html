DDI Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

DDI is a dice progression game for Apple Watch and iPhone. This privacy policy explains what information the app handles.

Data Collection

DDI does not collect, sell, share, or track personal data. The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Game Data

Game progress, settings, statistics, and unlocks are stored locally on your devices. The iPhone companion app may receive game status from the Apple Watch app through Apple system features such as WatchConnectivity.

Purchases

DDI may offer an optional Supporter in-app purchase. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. DDI does not receive or store your payment card details.

Diagnostics

DDI does not collect diagnostic data directly. Any crash reports or diagnostics that may be available to developers are provided by Apple through Apple's own systems only if users have enabled sharing diagnostics with app developers in their Apple device settings.

Children's Privacy

DDI does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if the app changes how it handles data. The latest version will be available at this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact: ddi.app.contact@gmail.com.

Because DDI does not collect or store personal data, there are no user accounts, server-side profiles, analytics records, or personal data records for the developer to access, export, or delete.
